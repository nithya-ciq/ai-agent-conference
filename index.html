  • Lead with the speaker's own published thesis or recent investment, not with our pitch deck.
  • Match check size to fund stage: don't pitch a Series B+ fund as if they'd lead our seed round; reframe as relationship-build for later.
  • Use architectural-peer language: when a partner has invested in a multi-agent or vertical-AI company, anchor the conversation on that pattern.
  • Default to 15-minute coffee asks in the conference venue; longer commitments are higher friction.
  • Capture every business card and conversation note for post-event follow-up within 48 hours of the event close.
